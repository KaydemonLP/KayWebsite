ABOUT ME

Welcome! Welcome!

I'm Kay! Often also known as KaydemonLP!
It seems that you've happened across my website, so let me introduce myself a bit.
I'm a young programmer, game dev and multimedia artist.

Despite my age I've got some projects behind me already, my proudest so far being Open Fortres.

Each day I try to progress my skills, be that programming, drawing, 3D modeling, VFX, animation, UX/UI design or perhaps learn something new!

Besides IT work I dabble a bit in handywork.
Primarily related to clothes, like sewing and knitting.

I mostly use it to modify clothes more to my liking, as the type of clothes and colors i like are hard to find!

However, I do on occasion create things fromscratch, for example, plushies, or my favourite, cosplay!

I tend to make things by hand, though I do have a sewing machine, which is handy.
